Document Scanner – Scan and Save as PDF

Document Scanner lets you easily scan documents using your phone camera and convert them into PDF files. The app allows you to manually crop the captured document to select the exact area you want before creating the PDF.

It is useful for scanning notes, assignments, receipts, and other paper documents quickly using your mobile device.

Key Features

• Scan documents using your phone camera
• Manually crop the captured image
• Convert scanned images into PDF files
• Save PDF documents directly to your device
• Simple and user-friendly interface
• Fast and lightweight scanning experience

Ideal For

• Students scanning notes and assignments
• Office workers scanning documents
• Saving receipts and paper records
• Creating PDF files from images

This app turns your phone into a convenient document scanner and helps you create PDF files anytime.
